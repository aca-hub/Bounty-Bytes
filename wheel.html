How to Send Money via M-Pesa Paybill

Follow these steps to deposit money into the game account:

  1. Open the M-Pesa app on your phone.
  2. Select Paybill from the menu.
  3. Enter the Business Number: 522522
  4. Enter the Account Number: 5173 3520 1317 5471
  5. Enter the Amount you selected above.
  6. Confirm all details are correct, then tap Send.
  7. You will receive a confirmation SMS from M-Pesa once the transaction is successful.

    8. After succesfull payment you will be re directed to the wheel.

Note: Always double-check the business number and account number before sending money to avoid errors.
