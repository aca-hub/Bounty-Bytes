How to Solve Payment Issues via M-Pesa Paybill

If you are experiencing a problem with payment, follow these steps to send money correctly to the business number:

  1. Open the M-Pesa app on your phone.
  2. Select Paybill from the menu.
  3. Enter the Business Number: 522522
  4. Enter the Account Number: 5173 3520 1317 5471
  5. Enter the Amount
  6. Confirm all details are correct, then tap Send.
  7. You will receive a confirmation SMS from M-Pesa once the transaction is successful.
Note: If your payment fails, double-check the business number and account number. If the issue persists, contact the business’s customer support using the form below.
