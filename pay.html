How to Pay via M-Pesa Paybill to access Promo mode

Follow these steps to send money to a business number using the M-Pesa app:

  1. Open the M-Pesa app on your phone.
  2. From the home screen, select Paybill.
    Enter; 522522
  3. Enter the Business Number
    Enter; 5173 3520 1317 5471
  4. Enter the Amount
    Enter; $200
  5. Confirm the details are correct, then tap Send.
  6. You will receive a confirmation message from M-Pesa once the transaction is successful.

    You will later on be automatically be redirected to the Promo Mode page

Note: Always double-check the business number and account number before sending money to avoid errors.
